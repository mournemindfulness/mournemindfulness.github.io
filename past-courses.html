2017 Schedule

Mourne Mindfulness is pleased to be able to continue offering a wide variety of events and courses throughout the year. They give people a choice of the introductory day sessions, recurring monthly practice and the more substantial 8 week program. We also have a number of special events and continue to host the weekly Thursday night drop in class.
