Deirdre McKibbin

Deirdre McKibbin is a Gestalt psychotherapist and Somatic Experiencing Practitioner (SEP), offering trauma‑informed body‑focused therapy across Ireland. As a member of Tairseach | Ireland SE™ (the national Somatic Experiencing association), she works with individuals seeking healing through SE’s bottom‑up, felt‑sense approach :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.

Somatic Experiencing® is a body‑based method developed by Peter Levine to resolve trauma and chronic stress held in the nervous system. It cultivates awareness of physical sensation, titration, and discharge to restore connection and resilience :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.

Situated within the Ireland SE™ community and training offerings (provided by IrelandSE.org), Deirdre offers one‑on‑one sessions supporting recovery from trauma, chronic tension, anxiety, and life transitions, helping clients safely reclaim their body’s natural capacity for ease and regulation :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.
