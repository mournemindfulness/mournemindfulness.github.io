About

Deirdre McKibbin is a Gestalt psychotherapist and trainee Somatic Experiencing Practitioner (SEP). She offers trauma‑informed, body-based therapy to individuals across Ireland. She is an active member of Ireland SE and engages in ongoing professional development to support clients using a somatic, nervous-system oriented approach.

What is Somatic Experiencing?

Somatic Experiencing® is a body-based therapeutic approach developed by Dr. Peter Levine. It is designed to help people resolve trauma, chronic stress, and overwhelm by gently guiding attention to internal sensations and supporting the body's natural ability to regulate and heal. Rather than retelling traumatic events, SE helps restore balance through attuned physical awareness, movement, and pacing.

Deirdre's Practice

Through her training with Somatic Experiencing Ireland (IrelandSE), Deirdre supports individuals in recovering from trauma, anxiety, chronic tension, and life transitions. Her work emphasizes safety, body awareness, and a compassionate therapeutic relationship. Sessions are suitable for those seeking to reconnect with their inner resources and move toward resilience and ease.

Deirdre also brings a background in Gestalt psychotherapy, which informs her relational, client-centered style. She offers sessions online and in person, and welcomes those exploring trauma healing for the first time.
