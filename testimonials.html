Testimonials

I completed the MBSR course with Deirdre last year in the wonderful surroundings of the Mourne Mountains.  Initially I was a bit worried about committing to 8 weeks as I was in the middle of moving house and I thought it would be difficult to ‘make the time’, but the programme became a real anchor for me.  I found the different meditation practices, such as the body scan and the 3 minute breathing space, a great way to help me switch off, slow down and ‘be in the moment’.

One of the great benefits for me was practising each week with other people and being given the space to honestly reflect on how my practice had been the week before.  Deirdre’s encouragement, guidance and support throughout the 8 weeks was brilliant.  We were able to contact her between sessions and she always helped us to find a way to embrace the practice in our day-to-day lives.

Since completing the course I have tried to practice on a daily basis.  There have been days when I avoid it and don’t make the time but then I feel out of sorts and that’s when I get back on my meditation stool and start again.
